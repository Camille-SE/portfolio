About

Hi, I'm Camille Joyce Bailey.
I was born in Bethesda, Maryland, USA.
I graduated from Old Dominion University with a degree in Communications. My career change to IT was made during the pandemic. I began by self-studying Python and algorithms on my own before enrolling in a coding bootcamp.

Throughout my time at General Assembly's Software Engineering Intensive Program, I learned the foundations for fullstack developer using Javascript, HTML and CSS. During my internship at Choyces I learned about design and teamwork. While working at Sentara I had the amazing opportunity to work as a Tier 1 Help Desk Customer Service Technician for doctors and nurses working from home during the pandemic.

I have special interests in AI, machine learning, languages and world cultures/affairs. I am currently a night owl student at Northern Virginia Community College, taking courses in computer science. My ultimate goal is to work for a company that I can continuously grow with and contribute to. I want to help my team change the world for the better.
